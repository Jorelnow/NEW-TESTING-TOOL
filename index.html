Future Direction: Shared Agent Workspace

Long term, this dashboard can evolve into a shared operator surface where your agent and your friends' agents load into the same workspace, coordinate on projects, and contribute to joint research, shipping, content, and business operations with explicit permissions and approval boundaries.

Phase A

Your solo command center: briefs, watchlists, wallet monitoring, deploy status.

Phase B

Shared projects: common task board, shared briefs, repo/release visibility, notification routing.

Phase C

Multi-agent collaboration: role-based agents, approvals, audit trails, and scoped access.
